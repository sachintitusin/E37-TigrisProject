About Tigris reserve: Guardians of the Wilderness

Welcome to Tigris Tiger Reserve, a sanctuary where the wild reigns supreme. Nestled in the heart of untouched nature, we are committed to the conservation of tigers and their habitat. Join us in our journey to protect these majestic creatures and experience the wonder of the wilderness.

  • #1 WWF 2024 Award

    Tigris Tiger Reserve was honored with the WWF 2024 Award for exemplary efforts in tiger conservation and habitat preservation.

  • 180 tigers saved

    Tigris Tiger Reserve proudly announces the milestone of saving 180 tigers, a significant triumph for global conservation efforts.

  • 2M+ visitors

    Over 2 million visitors have experienced the majestic beauty of Tigris Tiger Reserve, embracing nature and wildlife.

  • 14 Services

    Tigris Tiger Reserve offers 14 services, including guided tours, educational programs, and conservation workshops, enhancing visitor experience.
